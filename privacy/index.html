Privacy Policy

ASeek keeps indexing and search on your Mac.

Last updated: May 10, 2026

Overview

ASeek is designed to search files locally on macOS. ASeek does not upload file names, file paths, file contents, or search queries.

Data Stored Locally

ASeek may store the following data on your Mac:

  • Scan folders you choose
  • Folder authorization bookmarks provided by macOS
  • Preferences such as shortcut, theme, language, and ignored folders
  • Local index cache and recent item ranking data

Network Use

ASeek does not require an account and does not send your indexed file data to a server. If you contact support by email, your email address and message are used only to respond to your request.
